# Product

## Register

product

## Users
HR Manager (Nhân sự), Accounting Officer (Kế toán), and Business Employees (Nhân viên). Users access Odoo client via web browsers, requiring a highly legible, premium, responsive dashboard and clear form views to manage their day-to-day operations.

## Product Purpose
Calculate employees' attendance logs, automatically flag warnings, configure monthly salary rules, compute payrolls, and print pay slips with maximum correctness and premium UX/UI flow.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, professional, elegant. 3-word personality: Precise, Clean, Premium.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered layouts with generic heavy-gray borders or plain table fields.
- Hard-to-read warnings or success labels due to poor color contrast.
- Harsh animations, tacky bounce easing, or side-stripe border templates.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity**: Emphasize critical metrics with bento-style cards and clear typography hierarchy.
- **Dynamic Feedback**: Use smooth, layout-safe transitions and hover effects to guide user interaction.
- **Inclusive Contrast**: Ensure warning and status labels strictly satisfy WCAG AA contrast ratios (>= 4.5:1).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict WCAG AA contrast ratio compliance.
- Supports prefers-reduced-motion media queries to avoid animation fatigue.
